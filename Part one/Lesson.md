## ពាក្យថ្ងៃនេះ

### <span style="color: red;">爱</span> (ài)

**អត្ថន័យ:** love, like, be fond of

**ឧទាហរណ៍៖**
- 我<span style="color: red;">爱</span>你 (wǒ ài nǐ) - I love you
- 我<span style="color: red;">爱</span>吃中国菜 (wǒ ài chī zhōngguó cài) - I love eating Chinese food
- 他<span style="color: red;">爱</span>看电影 (tā ài kàn diànyǐng) - He loves watching movies

**ពាក្យពាក់ព័ន្ធ៖**
- <span style="color: red;">爱情</span> (àiqíng) - romantic love
- <span style="color: red;">爱人</span> (àirén) - spouse, loved one
- <span style="color: red;">可爱</span> (kě'ài) - cute, lovable
