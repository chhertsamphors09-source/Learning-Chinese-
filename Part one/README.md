## Part One

### 1. 演员 (yānyuán)
- **Part of speech:** noun
- **Meaning:** actor / actress
- **Khmer:** ត្រូវប្រែង

### 2. 主角 (zhǔjué)
- **Part of speech:** noun
- **Meaning:** main character / leading role
- **Khmer:** ត្រូវការបំពេញ

### 3. 剧情 (jùqíng)
- **Part of speech:** noun
- **Meaning:** plot / storyline
- **Khmer:** ដំណើរកិច្ច

### 4. 目的 (mùdì)
- **Part of speech:** noun
- **Meaning:** purpose / goal / objective
- **Khmer:** គោលបំណង
- **Example:** 他的旅行目的很明确。
- **English:** His travel goal is very clear.
