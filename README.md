# Lesson 1: Greetings 问候

## New Vocabulary 生词
| Chinese | Pinyin | English | Khmer |
|---------|--------|---------|-------|
| 你好 | nǐ hǎo | Hello | សួស្តី |
| 再见 | zài jiàn | Goodbye | លាហើយ |
| 谢谢 | xiè xie | Thank you | អរគុណ |
| 对不起 | duì bu qǐ | Sorry | សុំទោស |

## Dialogues 对话
A: 你好！(nǐ hǎo) - Hello!
B: 你好！(nǐ hǎo) - Hello!

## Grammar 语法
- 你 (nǐ) = you
- 好 (hǎo) = good
- 你好 = Hello (literally "you good")

## Notes 笔记
- 谢谢 can be shortened to 谢了 in casual situations
- Always smile when greeting in Chinese culture
